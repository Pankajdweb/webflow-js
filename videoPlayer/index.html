Universal Video Player

One Script. Every Source.

Pass any video URL via data attributes — YouTube, Vimeo, MP4, WebM — and get a beautiful, accessible player with zero dependencies.

MP4

MP4

Auto-detects MP4, generates a smart thumbnail (skips black frames), shows full controls with center play button.

Autoplay

MP4 — Autoplay

Perfect for hero backgrounds. Autoplays muted with loop, controls hidden.

YouTube

YouTube Video

Automatically detects YouTube URLs, fetches the high-res thumbnail, embeds the player on click.

Vimeo

Vimeo Video

Detects Vimeo URLs, fetches thumbnail via oEmbed API, embeds on play.

Quick Usage

<!-- 1. Include CSS & JS -->
<link rel="stylesheet" href="video-player.css">
<script src="video-player.js" defer></script>

<!-- 2. Add a player (YouTube / Vimeo / MP4 / WebM) -->
<div class="vp-wrapper"
     data-video-url="https://youtube.com/watch?v=..."
     data-title="My Video"
     aria-label="My Video player">
  <div class="vp-container"></div>
</div>

<!-- Autoplay hero -->
<div class="vp-wrapper"
     data-video-url="/hero.mp4"
     data-autoplay
     data-hide-controls>
  <div class="vp-container"></div>
</div>
data-video-url

Required. Any video URL (YouTube, Vimeo, MP4/WebM)

data-title

Optional. Accessible title (falls back to aria-label)

data-autoplay

Optional. Autoplays muted and loops (controls hidden)

data-hide-controls

Optional. Hides the custom controls
